A guided, step-by-step build. Caps and budgets are enforced — you can't exceed a per-attribute cap, a skill's tier max, or any point budget (your GM can override). Your character updates live on the right.

{# First-timer glossary — rulebook-accurate one-liners (Stormlight RPG Ch.3-6). #}
New to the Cosmere? — key terms
Ancestry
The species you descend from (human or singer); guides your look and grants ancestry talents.
Culture
The society you were raised in; grants its cultural Expertise.
Expertise
A field you know deeply — a language, craft, or place — that lets you attempt related tasks and gives an edge on them.
Heroic Path
Your training (Agent, Envoy, Hunter, Leader, Scholar, Warrior); grants a key talent, a free skill rank, and talent trees.
Talent
An ability or benefit gained through experience or training. A key talent tops each path and unlocks its specialties.
Investiture
Your Stormlight pool — the energy that fuels a Knight Radiant's surges.
Surge
One of ten powers over a fundamental force (gravity, healing, decay…). A Radiant wields the two of their order.
Radiant Order
One of ten orders, each bonded to a kind of spren, with two surges and a guiding philosophy.
Ideals
Five oaths a Radiant swears. Everyone's First Ideal is “Life before death…”; swearing it unlocks your surges.
Spren
A fragment of Investiture given life. A Radiant spren bonds with you to grant your powers.
Infected Art
Optional homebrew: an Invested disease that grants powerful Arts in exchange for a permanent cost. Most characters have none.
Identity

Who are you? Your ancestry grants bonus talents as you level; the culture you were raised in gives you its cultural Expertise. (Name and level live in the bar above.)

Singers gain Change Form (key talent) at level 1 and begin in a chosen form; a form’s bonuses apply while assumed.

Attributes

Spend your attribute points. 12 at creation, max 3 per attribute (hard cap 5; +1 every 3rd level). Defenses, health, and focus all flow from these.

Expertises

An Expertise is a field you know deeply — a language, a craft, a culture, a place — that lets you attempt related tasks others can’t and gives an edge on tests about it. You get 2 + your Intellect (your culture’s Expertise is added for you). One per line.

Heroic Path

Choose your heroic path. It grants a key talent and a free rank in its starting skill; then pick talents from its trees.

Optional homebrew. An Invested disease grants powerful Arts at a permanent cost. Each Art’s stat penalties (and boosts) apply to your character automatically — watch the card on the right — and its abilities appear on your sheet. Most characters have none; add one only with your GM.
