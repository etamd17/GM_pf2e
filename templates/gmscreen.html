Dying & Recovery

When reduced to 0 HP, move your initiative to directly before the creature that downed you, and gain Dying 1 (or Dying 2 if critical hit/failure).

  • Recovery Check: Flat check DC 10 + current Dying value.
    • Crit Success: Dying decreases by 2.
    • Success: Dying decreases by 1.
    • Failure: Dying increases by 1.
    • Crit Failure: Dying increases by 2.

When Dying reaches 0, you lose Dying and gain Wounded 1 (or increase Wounded by 1). If you take damage while Dying, increase Dying by 1 (or 2 for a crit).

Dead at Dying 4.

Elite & Weak Adjustments

Elite Adjustment (+1 Level)
  • +2 to AC, Saves, Attacks, DCs, Perception, Skills
  • +2 to Damage rolls
  • +10 to 30 HP (based on level)
Weak Adjustment (-1 Level)
  • -2 to AC, Saves, Attacks, DCs, Perception, Skills
  • -2 to Damage rolls
  • -10 to 30 HP (based on level)

Undead & Void Healing

  • Immunities Death effects, disease, poison, unconscious.
  • Void Healing Healed by void (negative) energy. Damaged by vitality (positive) energy.
  • Destroyed Automatically destroyed at 0 HP. (Do not use Dying rules for basic undead).
