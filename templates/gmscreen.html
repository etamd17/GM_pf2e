Common Conditions always-visible

Off-Guard
−2 circumstance to AC. (Replaces "flat-footed" from old PF2e.)
Frightened X
Status penalty equal to X on every check & DC. Decreases by 1 at end of turn.
Sickened X
Status −X to checks & DCs. Can't willingly ingest. Spend 1 action to retch (Fort save vs effect DC) to reduce by 1 (or 2 on crit).
Prone
Off-Guard. Attack rolls take −2 circumstance. Only move available is Crawl. Stand = 1 action.
Slowed X
Lose X actions at the start of your turn. Stunned X also makes you Slowed during recovery.
Stunned X
Lose actions at start of turn (up to your normal 3); Stunned reduces by amount lost. Also blocks reactions.
Wounded X
Increase Dying value by X+1 if you go down again. Removed by full Treat Wounds + 10-min rest, or Long Rest.
Concealed
Attacker rolls a DC 5 flat check before damage; failure = miss.
Hidden
Off-Guard to you. DC 11 flat check before damage. You know the target's square.
Undetected
Off-Guard to you. You don't know the square — must Seek or guess (DC 11 flat).

Dying & Recovery

At 0 HP: move initiative to just before the attacker, gain Dying 1 (or 2 on crit hit / crit failure on the save).

Save Result Tiers ±10 rule

Crit Success (beat DC by 10+ or nat 20) — no effect, or effect becomes Success.
Success — usually half damage; rider effects reduced.
Failure — full damage; rider effects apply.
Crit Failure (miss by 10+ or nat 1) — double damage, or worse rider effect.

Recall Knowledge DCs vs monster level

Common creature → use standard DC by level.
Uncommon → +2 to the DC.
Rare → +5 to the DC.
Unique → +10 to the DC.
Success = one piece of accurate info. Crit = two. Fail = nothing. Crit Fail = false info.

Earn Income / Task Level PF2e Core p.236

Trained = task at your level −2 to your level. Expert = +2 levels available.
Master / Legendary = +4 / +6 levels available; opens up "lucrative" tasks (3× pay) and city-level commissions.
Critical success = pay at task level +1. Failure = pay at task level −1. Crit fail = no pay, lose half a day.
